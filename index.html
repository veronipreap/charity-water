How to Play

Help Grey stop the drought witch by solving each hidden word before mistakes run out.

  1. Open the current chapter from the map and start the level.
  2. Tap letters on the keyboard to reveal the hidden word.
  3. Wrong letters increase mistakes and lower water level.
  4. Use hints when stuck (except final chapter, where hints are disabled).
  5. Solve all words in a chapter to unlock the next chapter.
  6. Keep going until the final chapter to complete the mission.
